About Me

Hi! I'm Idel Akbar Ramadan, known as Akbar Nawasunda in the music world. I'm a 19-year-old music producer, DJ, and composer based in Cipeundeuy, Bandung Barat, Jawa Barat.

Alasan gua mau jadi produser musik itu karena gua mau hidup gua itu ditulis dalam lagu. Lagu itu menurut gua ekspresi, emosi, dan pikiran yang disatukan menjadi sebuah keindahan dalam melodi.

I specialize in breakbeat, indobass, and jungle dutch bandung genres, always pushing the boundaries of sound and rhythm. My ultimate goal is to become a legendary musician who leaves a lasting impact on the music industry.
