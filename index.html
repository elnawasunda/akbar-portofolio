About Me

Idel Akbar Ramadan

My life is an ever-evolving song where every melody is an expression of emotions and thoughts united into beauty. As a music producer and composer, I'm dedicated to creating works that stir the soul.

I began my journey in music as a teenager, inspired by the energy of electronic music and the richness of Indonesian musical culture. With a unique approach blending breakbeat, indobass, and jungle dutch Bandung, I strive to create distinctive and captivating musical identity.
