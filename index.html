Tentang Saya

Idel Akbar Ramadan

My life is an ongoing song where every melody is the union of emotion and thought transformed into beauty. As a music producer and composer, I'm dedicated to creating works that stir the soul.

I began my journey in music as a teenager, inspired by the energy of electronic music and the richness of Indonesian musical culture. With a unique approach blending breakbeat, indobass, and Bandung's jungle dutch, I strive to create a distinctive and captivating musical identity.
